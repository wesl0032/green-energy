Three Ways to Go Green Today

Affordable Solar Panels

Solar energy has become one of the most accessible clean energy options for average households. Government rebates and financing programs has dropped the cost of installing rooftop solar panels by 70% in the past decade. The average 6 kW system can be installed for under $10,000 after incentives, and pays for itself within 6-8 years by reducing electricity bill cost. Community solar programs let house and apartment renters participate too.
